Reservation Information

  • Reservations requests must be sent 3 days in advance.
  • Only people with recommendation can enter into the Wedding reception room.
  • Babysitting room has capacity for 20 children from 3 - 36 months.
  • Breakfast buffet is open from 7 - 10 am.
  • Indoor Pool has tempered water.
  • You are free to bring your own food and use the kitchenette inside the rooms.
  • We are closed twice a year for 15 days; March from 1-15 and September from 15-30. Consider this information before making a reservation.
  • Always closed on Sundays and Mondays.
